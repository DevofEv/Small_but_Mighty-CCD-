Design Principles for Success

How to make civic engagement work

🌍 Design for Inclusion

Remove barriers: low voting age, no citizenship requirements, multilingual materials, stipends

🔍 Design for Transparency

Make processes open and understandable to build trust and accountability

🎯 Design with Clear Purpose

Define goals and extent of citizen influence from the start

💰 Design with Resources

Commit sufficient funding and support for genuine participation

🗣️ Design for Fair Discussion

Use skilled facilitators to ensure all voices are heard

📈 Design for Improvement

Evaluate and iterate to enhance effectiveness over time

Democracy is a Design Problem

When we design civic engagement with intention—prioritizing inclusion, transparency, and genuine power-sharing—we unlock the collective wisdom of our communities.

The power to build a better future resides in our communities, waiting to be unlocked.
